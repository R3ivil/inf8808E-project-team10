Independent analysis using Stack Overflow survey data

Developer AI adoption is high, but trust is uneven.

Built from the public 2025 Stack Overflow Developer Survey dataset, this guided D3 exploration compares developer profiles, work contexts, AI trust, frustrations, human-help needs, and agent readiness.

V4 - Frustration profile cards

The almost-right problem grows with use.

The top frustration is not only an onboarding issue. Daily users report almost-right AI outputs more often than occasional users, which points to review and debugging needs for mature adopters.

V5 - Human-help situations

Human judgment remains part of the workflow.

Even respondents who highly trust AI still prefer people for some tasks. This closes the story by showing where teams still need review, mentorship, and shared judgment.

Methodological notes

This independent class project visualizes derived aggregates from the public 2025 Stack Overflow Developer Survey dataset. Results are descriptive associations, not causal evidence. Missing and NA responses are excluded from chart denominators, and multi-select fields do not sum to 100%.
