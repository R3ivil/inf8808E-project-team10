The main frustration: AI that's close, but not quite right.

The top frustration is not only an onboarding issue. Daily users report almost-right AI outputs more often than occasional users, which points to review and debugging needs for mature adopters.

Methodological notes

This independent class project visualizes derived aggregates from the public 2025 Stack Overflow Developer Survey dataset. Results are descriptive associations, not causal evidence. Missing and NA responses are excluded from chart denominators, and multi-select fields do not sum to 100%.
